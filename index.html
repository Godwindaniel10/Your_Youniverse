Beginners Guide On Starting Your Spiritual Journey

by Jim Tolles

Starting your spiritual journey...

I really want to emphasize that all spiritual paths are sacred. We are all different, and we are all journeying towards different things in life. While ultimately we are all one, there is an importance in honoring our unique divine intelligence within us and how it moves us individually. To that end, this section is aimed to help you listen to your intuition/inner knowing as you build your spiritual practice.

A regular spiritual practice is key for most people to break out of unhealthy ego patterns and ignorance. In committing yourself to regular meditation and other spiritual practices, you can enrich your life and deal with adversity better.
