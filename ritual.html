A Simple Protection Spell

This simple spell comes from Jenna from The White Witch Parlour and is perfect for frequent physical and spiritual protection. It’s really easy to do and really great for centering and focusing your mind while providing general protection for you.

What You'll Need:

  • White sage
  • Star anise
  • Rosemary
  • Oil & burner / Cauldron
  • Black salt
  • Protection stones
  • Black candle
  • Mortar & pestle


STEP ONE: CAST A CIRCLE

Some start off spellwork by casting a circle. If this is you, begin to cast your circle now. You may like to smudge your space and stones/crystals to clear the energies that are present. Use the black salt to create a circle in your working space and place your stones in each quarter of the salt circle.


STEP TWO: PLACE BURNER/CAULDRON IN CIRCLE

You can use an oil burner with a tea light or an electric oil warmer whichever you prefer or have access to. Place your burner inside the salt circle and begin to warm your oil.


STEP THREE: GRIND YOUR HERBS

Add a few leaves of white sage and dried rosemary to your mortal and pestle and grind into a coarse mix. Attune yourself with the energies of these herbs as you smell and work with them.


STEP FOUR: ADD YOUR HERBS

Add your ground mixture of herbs to the oil and further take in their energies as you raise your own energy and apply deep intention to your spell of protection. Next, add the star anise in a manner that is meaningful to you.


STEP FIVE: AN INCANTATION

“I seek protection and sound my alarm My body, mind, and spirit now safe from harm My aura a shield to help me stay strong I now block negativity in all that is wrong”



Pagan and Wiccan Cleansing and Protection Spell
Pagans believe in the power of protective magic. This spell is referenced and used in several methodologies, and while it probably originated with the pagans, it is now used mostly for banishing all evil spirits from your home. This spell is more easily performed when you have a few trusted people with you to help.

What You'll Need:
  • A bowl of water
  • Sea salt
  • A smudging bundle of sage
  • 1 blue candle
  • Blessed oil (you can substitute rosemary oil)
  • Container (bowl or bag)


Starting at the front door and moving clockwise, smudge the perimeter of each room (be sure to get all corners and windows) in your home with the salt, the lit sage bundle, the candle flame and the water. In each room, use the oil to anoint all doors and windowsills; a smudge will do, or you may choose to use the oil to trace the shape of a symbol of your tradition such as a pentacle or a Saint Lucy eye charm.

You can say something simple like “May the goddess bless this home" or use a cleansing spell like:

“With the purifying power of water,

with the clean breath of air,

with the passionate heat of fire,

with the grounding energy of earth,

we cleanse this space.”

After you’ve done the cleansing ritual, ask your guests to put their token in the bowl or bag you’ve chosen, and put it in a place of honor in your home.


Old Magick – Fennel
A key element used for centuries to provide protection is fennel. This herb is actually a symbol of good, so burning it or wearing it will help keep black magic and other evil influences away. You can grow fennel in your own garden or buy it dried or as a fennel oil; either way, evil humans or spirits will recoil from it.


Phoenix Protection Spell


What You'll Need:
  • A bundle of feathers, either all white or in a variety of colors
  • 1 long, slender candle
  • 1 pot


Put the feathers in the pot, light the candle and use it to burn the feathers. When they have become ash, place your hands over the pot and say,

“Oh mighty fire bird come bless this ash.

Come through the planes,

through beast gates smash,

protect this place and all within.

Let us hear the fire bird sing.”

When you are through, smudge the ash on doorways and windows.
